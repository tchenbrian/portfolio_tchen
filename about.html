About

A little bit about myself, what I have done, and where my interests lie.

Background

I am a junior at Southwest CTA, a magnet high school that emphasizes project based learning and real world skills. In my upbringing, my family taught me three essential values: altruism, honesty, and hard work. I have maintained straight A's and a 4.0 unweighted GPA while taking several AP classes such as World History and English.

Experience

My current vocation is in design, both in web and graphics. In the course of two years, I have developed proficiency in graphic design and web design. I have successfully taken on several clients and have designed for commercial and educational purposes. In extracurricular, I am currently a member of SkillsUSA, a national organization that hosts competitions for skills such as web design. Finally, I have earned Adobe certifications in Illustrator and Photoshop.

Interests

As my years in design continue, I plan to incorporate my various interests into different websites. I have a profound interest in government and politics, for example. I can definitely see client work for political activism in my future. For hobbies outside of web design, I enjoy writing, computer gaming, and traveling.
